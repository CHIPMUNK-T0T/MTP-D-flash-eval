### 1. Infrastructure and Connectivity
Investors prioritize cities with robust digital and physical infrastructure that supports rapid scaling. Ensure high-speed fiber optics, reliable 5G coverage, and secure data centers are available to startups. Furthermore, develop logistics hubs and transport networks that reduce supply chain friction and lower operational costs for new ventures.

### 2. Talent Acquisition and Retention
Create a dynamic talent ecosystem by partnering with local universities to offer specialized, industry-aligned curricula. Implement aggressive recruitment incentives such as tax breaks, housing subsidies, and startup-specific stipends to attract top engineers and designers. Establish mentorship programs that connect local graduates with experienced founders to accelerate their growth trajectories.

### 3. Education and Workforce Development
Collaborate with regional institutions to establish incubators that provide hands-on training in emerging technologies and business models. Offer free or low-cost workshops and certification programs focused on the specific sectors where the city has a competitive advantage. Ensure that educational pathways are flexible and accessible to non-traditional candidates who may not have formal degrees.
